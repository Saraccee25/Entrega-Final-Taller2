Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of pets do you attend?

We care for exotic pets such as mini pigs, rabbits, goats, fish, birds, snakes, wolves...

How often should I bring my pet for a check-up?

We recommend bringing your pet for a check-up at least once a year. However, the frequency may vary depending on your pet's age, health condition, and lifestyle.

Do you offer emergency services?

Yes, we provide emergency veterinary services. In case of an emergency, please call our clinic immediately (during work shedule).

What is your shedule?

Our schedule is from Monday to Saturday, 8am to 9pm each day.
